A little bit about myself

I'm Sivakumar Chandrasekar, a Library and Information Science professional with a strong foundation in computer science. My journey began with a curiosity about how knowledge is organised, accessed, and used — which gradually evolved into a focused interest in digital libraries and information systems.

I have had the privilege of working with leading institutions such as the Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad (IIMA), Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA), and Indian School of Business (ISB). I focus on building simple, efficient, and user-centred solutions that make knowledge more accessible and impactful.
